# Lloyd's List

Historic maritime news and intelligence publication (founded 1734) covering shipping markets, risk/compliance, and security, with the Lloyd's List Shipping Podcast.

**Tier:** Freemium
**Auth:** None (some) / Subscription
**Last researched:** 2026-06-11

## Use cases

- Authoritative shipping news plus risk-and-compliance analysis (shadow fleet, sanctions, decarbonisation policy, IMO governance).
- The Lloyd's List Shipping Podcast — analyst-led discussion useful for atmospherics and trend-tracking.
- Deep-dive coverage by dedicated risk/compliance analysts (e.g. shadow-fleet evolution into 2026).

## Pricing

- **Free tier:** Some articles and the podcast are freely accessible.
- **Paid tiers:** Lloyd's List subscription for full editorial access — not publicly fixed (contact for pricing).
- **Enterprise:** Bundled with Lloyd's List Intelligence data products (see vessel-registry entry).
- **Notes:** Editorial publication; distinct from the Lloyd's List Intelligence data platform.
