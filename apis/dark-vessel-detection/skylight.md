# Skylight

Free maritime monitoring platform from the Allen Institute (originally Vulcan / Allen AI), providing AI-driven detection of illegal, unreported, and unregulated (IUU) fishing, dark vessels, and suspicious activity. Free for governments, NGOs, and qualifying organisations.

**Tier:** Free (for qualifying users)
**Auth:** Account login (app.skylight.earth)
**Last researched:** 2026-05-09

## Use cases

- Free dark-vessel detection and IUU fishing monitoring for the Strait of Malacca and adjacent EEZs (Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand).
- Vessel behaviour analysis (loitering, abnormal speed patterns, AIS gaps).
- Cross-platform integration — Skylight has 23+ API integrations and shares technology with partner platforms.
- Government and NGO maritime ops centres that can't afford Windward/HawkEye-scale spend.
- Education, research, and policy work on maritime crime in Southeast Asia.

## Pricing

- **Free tier:** Free access for governments, regional bodies, and NGOs working on maritime crime/ocean protection.
- **Paid tiers:** None — Skylight is a non-profit-funded platform, not a commercial product.
- **Enterprise:** Not applicable.
- **Notes:** Eligibility is decided by Skylight on a case-by-case basis. Commercial / for-profit users are typically not eligible.

## Authentication

Account login at app.skylight.earth. API access is gated and provisioned to approved organisations.

## Notes

- Skylight inherited Vulcan's maritime work after Paul Allen's death and is now operated by the Allen Institute.
- Strong fit if your organisation is government / NGO-aligned. Commercial operators typically use Windward / SynMax / Pole Star instead.
- Excellent partner for fusion alongside ReCAAP and IMB data when monitoring SOMS for IUU fishing-related activity (a common vector for unrelated maritime crime).
