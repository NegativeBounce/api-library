# Russell Group (ALPS Marine)

Specialty risk-intelligence platform (Russell ALPS) for (re)insurers, modelling aggregated and "clash" portfolio exposure across marine war, trade-event and political-violence scenarios by route, port, country and flag.

**Tier:** Enterprise
**Auth:** API Key / platform (licensed)
**Last researched:** 2026-06-11

> **Platform-first.** ALPS is a licensed exposure-management software platform with integrated data; access and any data integration are contract-based rather than a self-serve public API.

## Use cases

- Quantifying aggregated/clash war-risk and trade-event exposure across a marine portfolio by transit route, port, country, flag and commodity.
- Modelling the portfolio impact of a discrete event (port disruption, conflict escalation, chokepoint closure) — the kind of scenario your war-risk/insurance personas underwrite.
- Linking political-violence asset data to portfolio analytics for probabilistic political-risk events.
- Supporting capital optimisation and accumulation control for marine re/insurers.

## Notes

- Positioned as an unbiased third-party data/analytics layer across the (re)insurance value chain — strong for the **aggregation/accumulation** angle (e.g. "how exposed is my book if the Strait of Hormuz closes").
- Differs from Concirrus (per-vessel behavioural underwriting) by focusing on **portfolio-level** exposure and clash — complementary, not duplicative.
- Publishes notable event analyses (e.g. Red Sea disruption, port strikes) that can inform report narratives.
- Confirm whether a data API vs. platform-only access fits your build during licensing discussions.
