Specialist maritime intelligence, investigations, and response consultancy supporting shipowners, insurers, and legal teams with due diligence, incident response, and crisis support.

## Use cases

- Ship and counterparty due diligence (ownership, history, sanctions/deception exposure) for transactions and chartering.
- Maritime incident investigation and response (piracy, hijack, kidnap-and-ransom support, fraud, cargo crime).
- Crisis management and response retainers for shipowners/operators in high-risk trades.
- Bespoke maritime intelligence reporting and litigation support.
